DESIGNING A FOOD MENU APP

Background

Most food apps don't actually help people choose what to eat, or recommend restaurants and dishes based on their preferences and taste. They mostly suggest based on common taste for all, restaurant promotion regardless of the person's taste, etc. Noticing that was a big pain for the user, I decided to design a food menu app that focuses on the users need at that moment, health and dietary restrictions previously known by the app and helps environmentally and socially friendly restaurants (a big trend right now and also a personal value of mine).

Challenge

Design an iOS Food Menu app for restaurants that aims to help users quickly find the food (dishes) that best suit their needs.
